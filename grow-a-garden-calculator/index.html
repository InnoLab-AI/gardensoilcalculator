Understanding Plant Values and Mutations

In Grow a Garden, each plant has a base value that determines its selling price. This value can be dramatically increased through mutations (also called modifiers). Our calculator includes all known mutations with their exact multipliers, from common mutations like "Wet" (2x) to the legendary "Rainbow" (50x) mutation.

Mutations are special effects that can appear on your plants, significantly boosting their value. Here's how they work:

  • Common Mutations (2x): Wet, Chilled, Choc, Moonlit, Windstruck
  • Uncommon Mutations (3x): Pollinated, Sandy, Sauce, Pasta, Meatball
  • Rare Mutations (4-5x): Bloodlit, Burnt, Verdant, Plasma, Honeyglazed
  • Epic Mutations (8-12x): Fried, Static, Rot, Bloom, Frozen, Cooked

  • Exotic Mutations (120-190x): Galactic, Celestial, Disco, Voidtouched, Dawnbound
  • Special Mutations: Rainbow (50x), Gold (20x), Shocked (100x)

Grow a Garden Calculator FAQ

What is Grow a Garden?

Grow a Garden is a popular Roblox farming/gardening game where players grow plants, breed them for mutations, and sell them for profit. The game features hundreds of different plants and mutation combinations.

How do mutations work in Grow a Garden?

Mutations are special effects that can randomly appear on plants, multiplying their base value. Each mutation has a specific multiplier (like 2x, 10x, 50x) and rarity level. The final plant value is calculated using the mutation formula.

What's the difference between Rainbow and Gold mutations?

Rainbow is a 50x multiplier mutation while Gold is 20x. Both are extremely rare and valuable. Rainbow is generally considered the most desirable mutation due to its higher multiplier.

How does the friend boost work?

Friend boost is a percentage bonus applied to your final plant value. If you have a 25% friend boost and your plant is worth $1000, the final value becomes $1250. This boost is applied after all mutation calculations.

What are Foxfire mutations?

Foxfire and Corrupted Foxfire are the same mutation with a 90x multiplier. This is one of the most valuable Divine-tier mutations in the game. Harmonised Foxfire is even rarer with a 190x multiplier.

Is this calculator free to use?

Yes! Our Grow a Garden calculator is completely free with no registration required. Calculate unlimited plant combinations and values.

How accurate are the plant values?

All data is gathered through extensive in-game testing. We don't use any game code, only player-verified values. The calculator uses the exact same formulas as the game.

What's the most valuable plant in the game?

The most valuable base plants are typically the Exotic tier plants like Cyclamen ($3.2M base value). However, any plant can become extremely valuable with the right mutation combinations.

How do I get better mutations?

Mutations appear randomly when plants are fully grown. Some breeding combinations and plant care methods may increase mutation chances, but the exact mechanics are part of the game's mystery.

Can I save my plant calculations?

Yes! Use the "Add Plant value to list" feature to build a list of multiple plants and track your total garden value. This is perfect for planning garden layouts and profit optimization.

What's the Value to Weight mode?

This mode lets you input a target value and calculates what weight your plant needs to be to reach that value. It's useful for planning breeding goals or estimating plant requirements.

Do pet abilities affect plant values?

Pet abilities can provide various bonuses including increased plant growth speed, mutation chances, and value multipliers. Our pet calculator helps you plan pet leveling and hatching strategies.
